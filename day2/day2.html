Healthy Foods

Lean meats, healthy grains, and fruits are important for a well balanced diet. Chicken breast is high in protein while being low in saturated fats. Avocados are rich in important nutrients and contain healthy monosaturated fats. Chickpeas are high in fiber and provide a source of sustainable energy. Eggs are also high in protein while having healthy fats.
